Acoustic Guitars vs Electric Guitars

Acoustic guitars use the hollow body of the guitar to amplify the sound produced by the strings.
They do not need and amplifier to be heard but they have a maximum volume that they can produce.

Electric guitars rely on electronics to sense the vibrations of the string.
The signal produced by the electronics is very weak so electric guitars need an amplifier to magnify the guitar's.
Without the amplifier, It is hard to hear an electric guitar.
